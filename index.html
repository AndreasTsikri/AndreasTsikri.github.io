Intro

My name is Andreas and this is my static website. The site was completely developed on basic HTML, CSS and vanilla JS. For CSS, some bootstrap components was used. I built this site for 3 reasons:

  • 🎓 To demonstrate my knowledge on simple web design, post my previous projects on different technical fields (outside from github!) and some more personal info. In general, to have a more concrete resume page!
  • ☕ To have some fun in the proccess of building a well-formed, more detail-specific resume!
  • 🎯 To complete the week-8 assignment on CS50x-2022 Harvard online open course (Intro to Computer Science)!

I am a graduate Mechanical Engineer specialized in energy sector. I used to code before getting into the university. I made more complex projects at university, as NTUA is known for his wide spectrum of knowledge that brings to the table. The programs were more orientated towards solution of comlex mathematical equations. Some of them were more basic like sum of Series. We had also a UNIX course at first year. The main language that we used on the bigger part of coding assignments was FORTRAN, a little bit old but fast laguage for computational tasks. I fell in love with computer science at 2017. I love a lot of domains and concepts about machine learning, Turing machines, automata, game of life, open-source etc. As i dove deeper into the field, the more i got in love with computers and computer science. My final diploma thesis had a tone of coding in multiple languages and i also inserted some knowledge that i self-taught to myself from ML field. More on the projects on my project page!

Love working as a software engineer or a Data Scientist and improving my CS and development skills!

As final thoughts, CS connects the way of life that i want to follow (constant problem solving, communication and cooperation , do-things-on-your-own-way, imagination, creativity) along with the elegance of a very interesting and complex scientific field! **NOTE**:This site has been developed before Chatgpt!!
